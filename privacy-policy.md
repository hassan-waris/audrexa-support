# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: April 22, 2026**

Audrexa ("we", "our", or "the app") is a music player for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Audrexa collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

**Audrexa does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.** All your music files, playlists, metadata, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

### Data Stored on Your Device

- Music files you import into the app
- Playlist data and favourites
- Album artwork (cached locally)
- EQ presets and app preferences
- Purchase status (verified through Apple's StoreKit)

### Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
- We do not track your location
- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs
- We do not share any data with third parties

## In-App Purchases

Audrexa offers optional premium features via Apple's In-App Purchase system. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

## Third-Party Services

Audrexa may connect to the following services to fetch metadata and album artwork for your music library:

- **Deezer API** — to search for album artwork
- **MusicBrainz API** — to search for album metadata and artwork
- **iTunes Search API** — to search for album metadata and artwork

These requests contain only the song title, artist name, and album name from your music files. No personal or identifying information is sent.

## Children's Privacy

Audrexa does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation or any personal data to function.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and biometric locks.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
